Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and landscape maintenance.

How often should I water my lawn?

Most lawns need 1-1.5 inches of water per week, either from rainfall or irrigation. Water deeply and infrequently to encourage deep root growth.

When is the best time to prune trees?

The best time to prune trees is during late winter or early spring when they're dormant. However, some species may have specific timing requirements.

How do I maintain my irrigation system?

Regular maintenance includes checking for leaks, adjusting sprinkler heads, and seasonal system startup/shutdown. We recommend professional inspection annually.

What should I do about weeds?

Prevention is key - use pre-emergent herbicides in early spring. For existing weeds, use appropriate post-emergent treatments and maintain healthy turf to prevent weed growth.
