Seasonal Landscaping Tips

Expert advice for maintaining your landscape throughout the year

Spring Maintenance Tips

Lawn Care

  • Start mowing when grass reaches 3 inches
  • Apply pre-emergent weed control
  • Overseed bare patches
  • Begin regular fertilization schedule

Plant Care

  • Prune winter-damaged branches
  • Plant new trees and shrubs
  • Divide perennials
  • Start annual flower beds

Irrigation

  • Check and repair irrigation systems
  • Adjust watering schedules
  • Test rain sensors
  • Clean sprinkler heads

Summer Maintenance Tips

Lawn Care

  • Mow at higher height (3-4 inches)
  • Water deeply and infrequently
  • Monitor for pests and diseases
  • Keep mower blades sharp

Plant Care

  • Deadhead flowering plants
  • Monitor for pest activity
  • Fertilize container plants
  • Prune spring-flowering shrubs

Water Management

  • Adjust watering for heat
  • Check for leaks
  • Monitor soil moisture
  • Consider drought-tolerant plants

Fall Maintenance Tips

Lawn Care

  • Overseed cool-season grasses
  • Apply fall fertilizer
  • Keep leaves cleared
  • Reduce mowing frequency

Plant Care

  • Plant spring bulbs
  • Protect tender plants
  • Clean up garden beds
  • Divide overgrown perennials

Winter Preparation

  • Winterize irrigation systems
  • Clean and store tools
  • Protect sensitive plants
  • Plan spring projects

Winter Maintenance Tips

Snow Management

  • Clear snow from walkways
  • Protect plants from salt damage
  • Remove heavy snow from branches
  • Keep paths clear and safe

Plant Protection

  • Monitor for winter damage
  • Protect sensitive plants
  • Check for pest activity
  • Prune dormant trees

Planning

  • Review landscape design
  • Order spring plants
  • Maintain equipment
  • Plan spring projects
